About TST Civil Engineering

Building a Better Future, Together.

Our History

TST Civil Engineering was founded in 1985 by Thomas S. Thompson, a visionary engineer with a passion for innovation and sustainable development. Starting as a small firm with a handful of dedicated professionals, TST quickly gained a reputation for excellence in project delivery and client satisfaction.

Over the years, we have expanded our services and expertise to encompass a wide range of civil engineering disciplines, including structural design, transportation engineering, environmental consulting, and construction management. Our commitment to quality and innovation has allowed us to successfully complete numerous landmark projects across the region.

From the initial design phase to the final construction stage, we work closely with our clients to ensure that their vision is realized with precision and efficiency. Our team of experienced engineers, project managers, and support staff are dedicated to providing exceptional service and delivering results that exceed expectations.

Today, TST Civil Engineering stands as a testament to the power of hard work, dedication, and a unwavering commitment to excellence. We are proud of our history and excited about the future, as we continue to build a better world, one project at a time.

Our Mission and Values

Our Mission

To provide innovative and sustainable civil engineering solutions that enhance the quality of life for communities and protect the environment. We strive to exceed our clients' expectations through exceptional service, technical expertise, and a commitment to excellence in all that we do.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical principles.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and continuously seek new and better ways to solve complex challenges.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to designing and building projects that minimize environmental impact and promote long-term sustainability.
  • Collaboration: We foster a collaborative environment where teamwork, communication, and mutual respect are valued.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all aspects of our work, from project planning to execution.
